William Henry Gates III (born October 28, 1955) is an American business magnate, software developer, investor, author, and philanthropist. He is the co-founder of Microsoft Corporation. During his career at Microsoft, Gates held the positions of chairman,chief executive officer (CEO), president and chief software architect, while also being the largest individual shareholder until May 2014. He is considered one of the best known entrepreneurs of the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s.

  1. Bill Gates and Melinda French Gates, now divorcing, chair the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, the world's largest private charitable foundation.
  2. On May 3, 2021, Bill and Melinda each announced on Twitter they were ending their marriage after 27 years. They will still co-chair the foundation.
  3. Gates, who cofounded Microsoft with Paul Allen (d. 2018) in 1975, has transferred at least $3 billion worth of shares in public companies to Melinda.
  4. As of March 2020, when Gates stepped down from the Microsoft board, he owned about 1% of the software and computing company's shares.
  5. He has invested in dozens of companies including Canadian National Railway and AutoNation, and is one of the largest owners of farmland in the U.S.
  6. To date, Gates has donated $35.8 billion worth of Microsoft stock to the Gates Foundation.
